Infinity Search Pro (Coming Soon)

What It Is

Infinity Search Pro is a desktop application for Infinity Search that will have integrations from several more websites and the ability to manage your own custom settings that can be saved locally to your computer.

Why Use It

This application is meant to change the way that people think about accessing information on the internet. Infinity Search Desktop is designed in a way that makes the user experience better than any other service out there. Since the search engine will also be a desktop integration, it has additions such as custom shortcuts and having settings that are saved locally to make the experience even more efficient and enjoyable.
